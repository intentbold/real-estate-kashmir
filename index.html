Our Founder

Sheikh Asif

The founder of Real Estate Kashmir, Sheikh Asif, is an enthusiastic investor, builder, and entrepreneur committed to changing the Kashmiri real estate sector. He has established a reputation for trust, creativity, and customer-focused solutions because to his keen understanding of real estate investments and construction.

Sheikh Asif founded Real Estate Kashmir with the goal of assisting people in making more informed investments by streamlining the purchasing, selling, and construction procedures in the area. His goal is to enable everyone to profit from and have access to real estate.

Beyond business, Sheikh Asif helps people thrive by sharing his expertise on real estate investing and financial success. Real Estate Kashmir is still moving forward under his direction, one prosperous project at a time.
